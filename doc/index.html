Gotta be easy

Elastic is built for simplicity, and flexibility, that is why you dont get tons of classes to do your job. You get a base set of classes and a set of helpers to combine and produce great results in no time. Helpers are not intrusive, they are automagically run reading your class names.

How Many classes?

unit, column, two-columns, three-columns, four-columns, container. Hey you dont really need more than that in most cases. but if you do, you can combine the classes provided to make many combinations, its likely that a combination will do the trick.

Is that all?

Well actually no. There is same-height, full-height, also no-print, print-only, and no-print-content. But they are either helpers or for printing, and they are so easy to remember. now that is all.

What can i do with this?

Well keep reading to find out. But you can do elastic, fixed, liquid, and mixed layouts. really? r. really

Elastic Layouts

Elastic layout are accomplished by setting the max-width, min-width for div.layout in skin.css file

Fixed Layouts

Just override div.layout width to an ammount of pixels in skin.css and you got it fixed

Liquid Layouts

Liquid is the default mode

Mixed Layouts

Some parts fixed, some elastic? Well read tutorial for complex layouts to find out
